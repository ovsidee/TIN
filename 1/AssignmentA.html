Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi is a family of wireless network protocols based on the IEEE 802.11 family of standards.

It is commonly used for local area networking of devices and Internet access, allowing nearby digital devices to exchange data by radio waves.

These are the most widely used computer networks, used globally in home and small office networks to link devices.

Wi-Fi also provides Internet access with wireless routers and wireless access points in public places such as coffee shops, restaurants, hotels, libraries, and airports.

Wi-Fi has become an essential technology for modern connectivity.
